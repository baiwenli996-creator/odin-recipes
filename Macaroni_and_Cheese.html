Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese is a comforting dish made with cooked macaroni pasta and a creamy cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter (for topping)
  • 1 cup bread crumbs (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook macaroni according to package directions; drain.
  3. In a saucepan, melt 1/4 cup butter over medium heat.
  4. Stir in flour and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Gradually whisk in milk and cook until thickened.
  6. Remove from heat and stir in cheddar and Parmesan cheeses until melted.
  7. Add cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and mix well.
  8. Pour into a greased baking dish.
  9. In a small bowl, melt 2 tablespoons butter and mix with bread crumbs.
  10. Sprinkle bread crumb mixture over the macaroni and cheese.
